Indirect Speech

We often use a tense wich is "further back" in the past than the tense originally used. This is called "blackshift". We also may need to change other words that were used, for example pronouns.

1. When we backshift present simple changes to past simple
*I travel a lot in my job --> Hector said that she travelled a lot in her job

2. Present continuous changes to past continuous
*The baby's sleeping! --> He told me the baby was sleeping

3. Present perfect to past perfect
*I've hurt my leg --> She said she'd hurt her leg

4. Past simple changes to perfect past simple
*We lived in China for five years --> She told me they'd lived in China for five years

5. Past continuous changes to past perfect continuous
*It was raining all day --> He told me it had been raining all day

6. Past perfect doesn't change
*I had gone to the mall --> She said she'd gone to the mall


NOTE: If what the speaker has said is true or is relevant, it's not always necessary to change the tense. These might happen when the speaker has used a present tense.
*I go to the gym next to your house --> Jenny told me that she goes to the gym next to my house. I'm thinking about going with her.
